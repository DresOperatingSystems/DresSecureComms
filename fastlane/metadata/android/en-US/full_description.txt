DresSecureComms is a FOSS, de-Googled, all-in-one secure communications app.
It works fully offline for SMS and calls, with no Google services and no trackers.

Features:

* Messages: a private offline SMS client. Send and receive normal texts, or turn on
encryption per message. Encrypted messages use AES-256-GCM and only work between two
people who both use this app and share the same key (set by hand in Settings).
* Calls: dial any number or call a saved contact over your normal carrier line.
* Contacts: an encrypted local vault, stored with the Android Keystore. Add contacts by
hand or import them from the device.
* Threat Scan: check a URL against VirusTotal using your own free API key.
* Metadata Wipe: strip GPS and EXIF data from a photo before you share it.
* Geo Spoofer: set a mock GPS location, or have the app pick a random one.
* App Lock: biometric or device PIN.
